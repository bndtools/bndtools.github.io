## Join us at OCX 2026 in Brussels

We’re excited to share that the Bndtools team will be presenting at Open Community Experience 2026 next week in Brussels.
OCX is the Eclipse Foundation’s flagship open source conference, bringing together developers, architects, researchers, and industry leaders for three days of collaboration and technical exchange ([newsroom.eclipse.org][1]).

### Our session Bndtools 7.2.0 and Beyond

In our session (23 April 2026 14:00-14:15 CET) covers the latest improvements in bndtools / bnd which happened during the last two years since our last presentation:
- Java 24/25/26 support
- Smarter bnd analysis & tooling
- New Eclipse + CLI workflows
- A first look at 7.3.0

Check out the full session details here: https://www.ocxconf.org/event/2026/agenda?session=a34d563a-8a47-4698-87f8-73290ce9222a&shareLink=true

### Why OCX?

OCX 2026 features around 150 sessions across multiple tracks—including tooling, AI, automotive, compliance, and research—making it a key meeting point for the European open source ecosystem ([newsroom.eclipse.org][1]).

If you’re attending, we’d love to connect—whether you’re already using Bndtools or just curious about modular development and OSGi.

See you in Brussels!
